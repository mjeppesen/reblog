Find Creative Commons Images — CC Search

Matthew Jeppesen

10 May 2021 • 1 min read

CC Search is a fairly impressive search engine for Creative Commons images (including public domain images).

Here is an example of a diving pelican image I found on the site:

It searches an amazing list of sites :

    • Smithsonian Gardens

    • Smithsonian Libraries
